PING!

International Notification Platform

How to Use Ping!

To become a user of PING, send a text to 646-768-8479. With a simple reply of “Subscribe”, you'll be subscribed to receive notifications of relevant information in your region. This automatic number system will help alert people for preparations due to severe weather conditions. To stop receiving messages, reply to the number with "Stop" or to resubscribe, reply to the number with "Start" and you'll receive notifications once again.
