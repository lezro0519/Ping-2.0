PING!

International Notifcation Platform

Our Mission

Our group looks to create a platform using the twilio API to help individuals living in third world/ underdeveloped countries stay informed and alert by sending messages. We plan to develop a system where we can send certain individuals messages (ex: when the next food/water/medical shipment from Unicef will arrive, if there are weather conditions that could be dangerous, or even nearby attacks) so people can be quickly updated on extremely important information. Furthermore, these messages would be based on the certain individuals location.

In 2010 Haiti was hit with a 7.0 Mw earthquake that resulted in catastrophic damage all over the nation. Prior to the earthquake, individuals in Haiti did not have a strong or affordable internet infrastructure. Thus, the nation's citizens had limited communication and knowledge of significant events. With PING! individuals could be notified when a natural disaster is about to occur and what safety precautions to take. Furthermore, PING! can help nations with the recovery and relief process by connecting people to essential resources such as food, shelter, medicine, and water.
